How It Works

Solar Panels for your home or business are a great investment. The sun causes the panels to generate DC current which is then converted to AC current by an inverter to be used inside your home. When an appliance calls for power the inverter will let current flow from your solar panels stopping the import of electricity from your supplier. If the solar panels are not generating enough power to run the appliance then the local grid will supply the balance.
