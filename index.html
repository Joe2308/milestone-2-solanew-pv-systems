Choose Renewable Energy

Solar Panels for your home or business are a great investment. The sun causes the panels to generate DC current which is then converted to AC current by an inverter to be used inside your home.... When an appliance calls for power the inverter will let current flow from your solar panels stopping the import of electricity from your supplier. If the solar panels are not generating enough power to run the appliance then the local grid will supply the balance.

Try Our Solar savings Calculator!

We know one of the most important questions you will have for us is "how much can I save?". We have created a very user-friendly savings calculator that will give you an accurate calculation on savings for your location. To find out more about how solar panels work, check out this great video.

Savings Calculator Instructions:

  1. Choose your county. This lets us know the average sunshine per metre squared for your location.
  2. Choose your roof orientation. Solar panels work best on south facing roofs, but also work well on east and west configurations. Solar panels do not work effectively on north facing roofs.
  3. Enter an approximate value of free space on your roof in meters squared.
  4. Enter what you are currently paying per unit of electricity in cents. The savings will change based on the information you input.
